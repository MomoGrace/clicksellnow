Split PDF Files Online for Free

Our Split PDF tool helps you separate a PDF document into individual pages. This is useful when you only need certain pages from a long document, report, worksheet, form or scanned file.

The tool works directly in your browser. Your file is processed on your device, making it simple and convenient to use.

Why Split a PDF?

  • Extract specific pages from a large PDF file.
  • Separate scanned documents into individual pages.
  • Share only the pages you need.
  • Organize reports, forms, worksheets and documents more easily.

How to Split PDF Online

  • Choose a PDF file from your device.
  • Click the Split PDF button.
  • The pages will be separated into individual PDF files.
  • Download the ZIP file containing the split pages.
